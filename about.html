WHY SGP EXISTS

FORMALIZING THE POST-BEIJING POWER SHIFT.

The demand for gender expertise exploded after the **1995 Fourth World Conference on Women in Beijing**, when governments committed to gender mainstreaming. Specialists were hired globally by every major institution.

As the field grew, the organization and resources did not. New specialists lacked networks, mentorship, and standardized frameworks. SGP was created to formalize, organize, and standardize this profession, ensuring every member has the resources and intergenerational knowledge needed to **thrive and win.**
